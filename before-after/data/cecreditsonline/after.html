About

What is CE Credits Online?

CE Credits Online, a dba of The Riesling Group, Inc., has been creating and providing graduate-level professional development courses for K-12 educators since 2001.

We create timely, relevant, research-based courses — from 21st-century teaching practices to classroom management and social-emotional learning — built to have an immediate impact in the classroom.
