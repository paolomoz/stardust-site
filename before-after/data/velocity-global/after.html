FAQs

The questions a board asks before approving global expansion, answered.

How does Pebl ensure compliance with local labor laws in 185+ countries?+

Our regional legal teams monitor regulatory shifts, flag issues before they become problems, and keep every EOR engagement up to date. We're also backed by Baker McKenzie, the #1 global employment law firm. That legal depth is a big reason our platform is rated #1 for compliance on G2.

How does Pebl prevent worker misclassification when hiring internationally?+

With Pebl as the legal Employer of Record, your hire is properly classified under local law from day one. You maintain the day-to-day working relationship; we bear the legal responsibility for appropriate classification.

How does Pebl's pricing compare to setting up a local entity?+

Setting up an entity can cost five to six figures and take months. Our flat per-employee monthly fee is significantly lower, and our EOR services let you onboard in as little as 24 hours. You trade high, uncertain costs for speed and predictability.

How do Pebl's EOR services reduce legal and financial risk?+

Pebl owns the legal employer responsibility, so liability for global compliance rests with us rather than your organization. You keep full control of the work and the relationship; we hold the legal exposure across 185+ countries, backed by in-country experts.
