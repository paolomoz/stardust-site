Accessibility assistance at JFK

JFK offers a range of accessibility features and services designed to make every part of your trip through our terminals accessible and comfortable. For additional assistance—such as mobility support or help navigating between gates, terminals, security, or customs—you may also wish to arrange support in advance through your airline.

Nominate an employee for great service

Did an employee at JFK make an impact? JFK participates in the WE SOAR program. When you nominate an employee for their hospitality and hard work, they could receive a $25 gift card and a chance at a quarterly reward!

Convenient pick-up and drop-off areas

Need to wait for your passenger? The on-airport pickup and drop off lot at Lefferts Boulevard AirTrain station is now available. It is the perfect alternative for passengers to be picked up or dropped off and avoid curbside congestion.

JFK customer support on WhatsApp
