The consultation questions

Six areas we want your views on.

The full questionnaire spans these six areas. Each question links to the relevant section of this document and to the supporting Heathrow Expansion publications.

3 questions

Masterplan

Tell us what you think about the Preferred Masterplan and the components that make it up — boundary design, displaced facilities, landscape and noise.

1 question

Construction

Tell us what you think of our construction proposals and the ways we are proposing to minimise effects on communities and the environment.

3 questions

Future Operations

Tell us what you think of our runway alternation proposals, the ban on scheduled night flights, and our proposals for managing early growth.

3 questions

Surface Access

Tell us what you think about public transport access and the Heathrow Ultra-Low Emission Zone and Vehicle Access Charge.

1 question

Managing the Effects of Expansion

Tell us what you think about our proposals to manage the environmental effects of expansion.

Other comments

Next steps

What happens after the consultation closes.

Following the close of this consultation we will review our proposals, taking into account all feedback alongside our ongoing evaluations.

Review feedback

Following the close of the consultation we review all feedback alongside our ongoing evaluations and engagement findings.

Refine proposals

We refine our masterplan and our proposals to build and operate a new runway.

Finalise documents

We finalise our Environmental Statement and a range of supporting technical documents.

Publish Consultation Report

We publish a Consultation Report setting out the feedback received, our response, and how it has been considered.

Submit DCO application

We submit our application for a Development Consent Order to the Planning Inspectorate in 2020.

Runway opens

Subject to consent, our new runway opens in around 2026.
