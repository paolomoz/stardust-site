100 years

Passion for high-quality power tools.

Manufacturing tools for professional tradespeople is one of the most demanding industries — and we have been doing it for 100 years. From the first portable chain mortiser in 1925 through the EC-TEC brushless motor era, every Festool tool is engineered to be repairable, accurate, and quiet on dust.

18 V system

Simply clever – the Festool 18-volt system.

One battery platform across cordless saws, sanders, drills, dust extractors, lighting, and accessories. Designed so a tradesperson can move from morning cuts to afternoon finishing without changing kit. The Tabless cell architecture in the latest battery generation extends runtime under load and shortens charge cycles.

Tools for the toughest demands. Engineered in Wendlingen, Germany.
