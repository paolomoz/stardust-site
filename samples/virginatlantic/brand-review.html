Brand review — Virgin Atlantic

Descriptive snapshot of https://www.virginatlantic.com/ as captured by stardust:extract on 2026-04-28. The colours, fonts and motifs below are pulled from the live site and are the constraint for any brand-faithful refresh.

Tensions

Hero relies on photo + photo-anchored card for legibility

The H1 ("Take off to the UK and beyond") sits on a white card pinned over the photograph; the booking widget sits directly on the purple chrome. Contrast is fine on the captured frame but the pattern is fragile to image variation. Any refresh that loosens the headline-card structure must keep contrast guaranteed.

Two unrelated H2 sizes (24 / 40)

Section headers ship at both 24 px and 40 px with no intermediate step, which makes the "Fly in style" and "Flying with us" headings feel like a different typographic system from "Even more flights across the Atlantic" or "Pre-travel advice". A consolidated heading rhythm is the cheapest legibility win in a refresh.
