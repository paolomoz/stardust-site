Our programs · choose what to learn about

Three doors. Each opens to a brief explanation in the same place — no new page until you're ready.

Emergency Shelter+

A safe place to sleep tonight, every night, for individuals and families.

  • Family resource centers and individual shelters across Salt Lake County
  • Open intake; no appointment required for emergency shelter
  • Connection to case management and supportive services from day one
Supportive Services+

Case management, employment, mental and physical health navigation while a person is sheltered.

  • One case manager, walking alongside
  • Employment readiness and connection to local employers
  • Health navigation, including the Medically Vulnerable People program
Housing Assistance+

Rapid re-housing, supportive housing, and the patient navigation that holds.

  • Housing-first: housing comes before conditions are met, not after
  • Supportive housing in communities like Palmer Court
  • Rapid re-housing with assistance for first months of rent and stabilization
