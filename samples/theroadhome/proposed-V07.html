What the year held

In the last reporting year we assisted 3,491 people with employment and housing-related services. Of those who shelter with us, about 60% receive case-management services at some point during their stay. On any given night, 1,550 or more of the people we work with are not in shelter at all — they are in housing.

The numbers describe a method, not a miracle: housing-first, progressive engagement, rapid re-housing, housing navigation — and the institutional patience to keep doing them, in this town, for a hundred years.
