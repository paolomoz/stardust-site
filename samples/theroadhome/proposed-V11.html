Ray.One of the people The Road Home works with — drawn from the source archive in the same composite-illustrative spirit the original uses.

Ray came in off the street one winter and stayed in shelter long enough that the staff knew his coffee order. He stayed long enough that he learned theirs.

What changed was not the shelter, in the end. It was the apartment that came after. Palmer Court — a hundred and fifty units of permanent supportive housing, with case management down the hall, in a building TRH ran on the principle that housing comes first and the rest can be sorted from there.

That principle is the field's name for it: housing-first. For Ray it is just where he lives now.
