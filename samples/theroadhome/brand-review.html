06 — Tensions

What a refresh should address

Mechanical readings of the captured surface that point at refresh opportunities. Descriptive, not prescriptive — the redesign target gets to decide which to act on.

Hero contrast risk

Tagline sits over a photographic background with no explicit dark-scrim layer. Contrast is implicit and viewport-dependent.

Hero IA collision

Tagline + 3 question-cards + photo + utility nav in one viewport. Hierarchy is unclear at a glance.

CTA vocabulary fragmentation

8 distinct CTA verbs on one page (Donate, Give, Read More, More Stories, Submit, Subscribe, Get Help, Get Involved). The verb set competes with itself.

Ad-hoc type scale

Heading sizes 60 / 45 / 20 / 18 / 16 px don't sit on a single modular ratio. The 45 → 20 jump is the largest discontinuity.

Two banner colours, one role

Purple banners (JOIN OUR TEAM, PROGRAMS) and teal banners (IMPACT, footer) both read as section-color but no clear semantic separates them.

Zero design tokens

The site ships 0 CSS custom properties at :root. Every style is local; the system forks under maintenance pressure.
