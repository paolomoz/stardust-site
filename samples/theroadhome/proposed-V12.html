Refuge. Resources. Relief.

The Road Home provides emergency shelter, supportive services, and housing assistance that help individuals and families step out of homelessness in Salt Lake County and along the Wasatch Front.

We have done this work since 1923. We do it with the practices the field calls housing-first, progressive engagement, rapid re-housing, and housing navigation.
