Where the numbers come from · three programs

The numbers above describe the work; these are the practices that produce them.

Emergency Shelter

A safe place to sleep, tonight, every night. Open intake. Connection to services from day one.

Supportive Services

Case management, employment, mental and physical health navigation while a person is sheltered.

Housing Assistance

Rapid re-housing, supportive housing, and the patient navigation that holds.
