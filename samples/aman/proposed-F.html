01The Cover Story

The Caribbean, slowed.

A peninsula reserve, eighteen pavilions, and one of the longest coral reefs in the hemisphere — a portrait of summer at Amanyara.

For two decades the Aman world has been built on a single instruction: arrive in a place, and let it set the pace. At Amanyara, the pace is set by reef weather and the angle of late-afternoon sun across the dunes. We spent a week there — here is what we found.

02The Programme

Three days, no clock.

A wellness reset curated with our global ambassador — recovery rituals, elite-athlete movement, and the silence of the cliffs above the Andaman.

The Detoxification Programme isn't a regimen, our ambassador will tell you, it's a permission. Three days at Amanpuri to step out of the chronograph, to learn the rhythm of the body again, and to do nothing on the schedule it is not asking for.

03The New & Noteworthy

Amangati sets sail.

Aman at Sea's first vessel begins her inaugural season this winter — fifty cabins, the Aman register, and a coastline rewritten daily by tide.

A ship is the most ambitious form of place a hotelier can choose. Amangati, Aman at Sea's flagship, opens for reservations.
