About this consultation

What we're consulting on, and why now.

This is Heathrow's statutory consultation on our preferred proposals for expanding the airport with a third runway. Following two earlier non-statutory consultations, we have developed a Preferred Masterplan, an indicative phased delivery, and a set of measures to manage the effects of construction and operation on local communities and the environment.

Your feedback during this period will directly inform the Development Consent Order (DCO) we submit to the Secretary of State for Transport.

  • Our Preferred Masterplan. The future layout of the airport: runway, terminals, road and rail access, parking, displaced land uses, and how growth will be phased.
  • Future operations. How a three-runway airport would be operated, including runway alternation and a ban on scheduled night flights.
  • Effects of expansion. Our preliminary assessment of effects on the environment, communities, health, transport, and the historic environment.
  • Mitigation and compensation. Property compensation, noise insulation, a Community Fund, and the measures that will manage construction and operating effects.

Foreword
Expansion is not just about minimising impacts, but about maximising opportunities — for the whole of the UK.

Since our airport operations began over 70 years ago, Heathrow has played a unique role in the development of the surrounding area, and we are proud of the partnerships we share with local communities. Today, we are committed to working closely with our neighbours to develop plans for an expanded airport and ensure that we deliver a fairer Heathrow for the next generation.
