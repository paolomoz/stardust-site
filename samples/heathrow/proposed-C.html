The 5-minute summary

Four areas. One consultation. Your views.

This consultation seeks your views on four key parts of our developed thinking for an expanded Heathrow.

Area 01

Our Preferred Masterplan

Our proposals for the future layout of the airport including the runway, terminals and road access.

Area 02

Plans to operate the future airport

How the future three-runway airport will be operated, including night flights and runway alternation.

Area 03

Assessment of effects of growth

Our preliminary assessment of the likely effects of expansion on the environment and local communities.

Area 04

Plans to manage the effects of expansion

Our proposals for mitigating the effects, including property compensation, noise insulation and a community fund.

“

Expansion is not just about minimising impacts, but about maximising opportunities; it will create thousands of new jobs and double the number of apprenticeships by 2030, giving local young people a launchpad as they set out on their careers.

John Holland-Kaye · Chief Executive · Heathrow
